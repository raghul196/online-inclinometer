About Us

Built for Precision

I am Raghul, the creator of PV Freund and this online inclinometer. This tool was born while solving a roof-pitch challenge for solar planning, but I realized every maker—from carpenters to DIYers—needs a high-precision angle finder. My mission is to build tools that are so intuitive you can skip the "how-to" and get straight to the "doing."

Utility Focused

Whether it's a roof pitch, a staircase, or a table saw blade, get the angle you need in seconds.

Frictionless UX

No bloat, no complex menus. I design my apps so you spend time on your craft, not learning the interface.

Pro-Grade Data

Uses raw MEMS sensor data for sub-degree accuracy. Private, 100% client-side, and hardware-accelerated.

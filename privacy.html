Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection

Our Inclinometer app accesses your device's accelerometer and gyroscope sensors to calculate surface angles. This data is processed locally on your device in real-time. We do not collect, store, or transmit this sensor data to any external servers.

2. Device Permissions

To function, the app requires "Device Orientation" permissions. This is used solely for the mathematical calculation of the pitch and roll of your device.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share any user information. If this app is hosted on a third-party platform (like GitHub Pages or Vercel), they may collect basic web logs (IP addresses) for security purposes.

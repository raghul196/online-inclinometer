How Our Online Inclinometer Works

This online inclinometer is a web-based application designed to measure surface angles with high precision, using the sensors already built into your smartphone or tablet. It transforms your device into a powerful digital spirit level, bubble level, or clinometer without requiring any software installation. Everything runs directly in your web browser.

Core Functionality

  • Real-time Measurement: Once you grant sensor access and press 'Start', the application reads data from your device's accelerometer and gyroscope to calculate the pitch (X-axis) and roll (Y-axis) angles in real-time.
  • High-Precision Bubble Level: The central graphic interface features a virtual bubble that moves based on the device's tilt, providing an intuitive visual guide to achieving a perfect level.
  • Zero Calibration: The 'Zero' button allows you to set any surface as a reference point. This is useful for measuring the angle of one surface relative to another, rather than to absolute level.
  • Unit Conversion: In the settings panel, you can switch between measuring in degrees (°) and percent (%).

Common Use Cases

This tool is perfect for a wide range of tasks, including:

  • Construction & Carpentry: Ensuring walls are plumb, floors are level, and beams are installed at the correct angle.
  • Solar Panel Installation: Optimizing energy generation by setting PV panels to the precise tilt angle for your location.
  • DIY Projects: Hanging picture frames, installing shelves, or assembling furniture with perfect alignment.
  • Photography & Videography: Leveling your camera for perfectly straight horizons in your shots.
  • Vehicle Maintenance: Checking the angle of components for alignment or during repairs.

100% Private and Secure

We are committed to your privacy. All sensor data is processed directly on your device by the browser. No information is ever sent to our servers. Your measurements are your own.

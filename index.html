How it Works

This online inclinometer uses your device's built-in sensors to measure surface angles (pitch and roll). Place your phone on a surface and press 'Start Measuring' to see the tilt in real-time. The 'Zero' button allows you to set the current angle as 0 for relative measurements. It's a handy tool for Solar-PV installation, leveling, construction, Wood work, DIY projects, and more.
